Cisco DevNet's Share Your Experience (SYE) is a hands-on initiative conducted at Cisco conferences where developers and customers provide direct feedback through interactive activities, using design thinking to understand community needs and inform product development. SYE is being upgraded to a hybrid experience at Cisco Live Las Vegas, incorporating gamified digital interactions alongside traditional tactile engagement.
